公司简介

慧真电子（东莞）有限公司，以“圆容明慧，真诚利他”为核心价值观，致力于整合本土资源，深耕LED工作灯/工业照明领域ODM制造。依托成熟的电子供应链及品控体系，拓展承接消费电子、智能硬件等相关领域的OEM&ODM项目合作。

秉承“第一次做正确”的理念，旨在通过规则，流程及基本遵守，为全球客户提供超越期望的价值与长远发展的动力，最终回馈社会，实现共生共赢。

COMPANY PROFILE

HuiZhen Electronics (Dongguan) Co., Ltd. adheres to the core values of "Comprehensive Wisdom, Genuine Altruism." We are committed to integrating local resources and focusing on ODM manufacturing for LED work lights and industrial lighting. Relying on a mature electronics supply chain and quality control system, we also extend our expertise to OEM & ODM projects in related sectors such as consumer electronics and smart hardware.

Guided by the principle of "Doing It Right the First Time," we aim to deliver value beyond expectations and provide sustainable growth drivers for our global clients through rules, processes, and fundamental compliance. Ultimately, we strive to give back to society and achieve mutual success.
